Privacy Policy

Effective Date: {{ .Year }}

1. Information We Collect

Webhook Data (User-Provided): When you send requests to a webhook URL, we collect:

  • HTTP headers
  • Query parameters
  • Request body
  • Method, timestamp, and metadata

This data is collected solely for debugging, inspection, and replay functionality. You should not send sensitive personal data or production secrets.

Account Information: If you register an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Hashed password
  • API key (system-generated)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Display webhook requests in your dashboard
  • Allow replay and inspection
  • Enable API access and security
  • Provide support and product updates

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Retention

Public (unauthenticated) webhook data is automatically deleted after a short retention period (e.g., 24–48 hours).
Authenticated users’ data may be retained until deleted by the user or the account is removed.

4. Cookies and Session Storage

We use cookies and sessions to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Protect against CSRF
  • Improve user experience

5. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect webhook and user data. However, due to the nature of webhooks, you should never send confidential or production data through this service.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • View and delete webhook logs associated with your account
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request support via our contact form

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

8. Contact
